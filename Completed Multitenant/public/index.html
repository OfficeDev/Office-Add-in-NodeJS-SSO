WELCOME
This add-in demonstrates how to use single sign-on for the Office host, the add-in, and Microsoft Graph.

To use this add-in

  • 1 Be sure there are some files in your OneDrive for Business (Office 365) account.
  • 2 Click the button. If you are not logged in to Office, you are prompted to login.
  • 3 When you are logged in, the files in your account are listed below.
